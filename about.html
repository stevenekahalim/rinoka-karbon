About Us

Leading the Way in Sustainable Coconut Charcoal Production

PT Rinoka Karbon Indonesia

PT Rinoka Karbon Indonesia is a premier manufacturer of high-quality coconut charcoal products, serving both domestic and international markets. With a commitment to sustainability and excellence, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner in the coconut charcoal industry.

Our state-of-the-art production facility utilizes advanced technology and environmentally responsible processes to transform coconut shells into premium charcoal products that meet international quality standards.

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality coconut charcoal products while maintaining sustainable practices that benefit our environment, communities, and partners. We strive to be the preferred supplier for businesses worldwide seeking reliable, eco-friendly charcoal solutions.

Our Vision

To become the leading coconut charcoal manufacturer in Indonesia and a globally recognized brand known for innovation, quality, and environmental stewardship in the renewable energy sector.
